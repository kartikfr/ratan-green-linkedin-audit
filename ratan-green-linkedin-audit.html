What's Not Working

Six issues behind the 3.5 score

Critical

No Proof Content Whatsoever

30+ MW installed across multiple states. ISO 9001:2015 certified. 12 years of delivery. Not one before/after bill, site photo, or client result appears in any post. For a company whose value proposition is "we deliver what we promise," the absence of proof is the costliest content mistake on this page.

Fix

One case study post every two weeks: state, system size in kW/MW, monthly savings in rupees, one site photo. Client name optional.

Critical

Posting Cadence Is Too Low to Build Algorithmic Reach

LinkedIn's algorithm rewards consistency. A page posting once every 1–2 weeks loses reach with every gap. In a trust-first category like solar EPC, infrequent posting signals inactivity, not credibility.

Fix

Minimum 3 posts per week, Tuesday/Thursday/Saturday. xto100x delivers a monthly calendar with every slot written and designed before the week starts.

Critical

Captions Have No Hook and No CTA

Every caption follows the same pattern: generic awareness statement, 2–3 lines, then a contact number or nothing. No hook, no question, no CTA matched to content type. Readers scroll past without engaging.

Caption structure every post needs
1
Hook: one line that stops the scroll
2
Context: what's happening and why it matters to the reader
3
Insight: a number, a position, or a lesson they can act on
4
CTA: matched to content stage: question for education, DM for leads
Critical

Founder Visibility Is Zero

Ajay Singh Shekhawat is an EQ 30 Under 30 awardee with 14+ years in solar. His face and name appear nowhere in the company's LinkedIn content. Founder-led posts consistently outperform branded content 3–5x on LinkedIn, making this the highest-return format RGESPL isn't using.

Fix

One founder-voice post per month, first-person: a position on policy, a lesson from a recent project, or a take on India's solar market. xto100x ghostwrites; Ajay posts.

High Priority

RGESPL Brand Mark Is Never Explained

The "RGESPL" acronym appears on every post but is never expanded. New visitors see a logo and a code with no context for what this company does, where it operates, or who it serves.

Fix

Rewrite the About section: full company name, services (EPC, O&M, rooftop, ground-mount), geographies, ISO certification, 12-year track record, follow CTA. Every post should spell out "Ratan Green Energy Services" at least once.

High Priority

No Video Content

Zero video in the analyzed feed. Solar EPC has compelling raw material: installation time-lapses, panel unboxing, inverter room walkthroughs, commissioning visits. Video drives up to 5x more engagement than static posts on LinkedIn, and RGESPL can produce it without a studio.

Fix

One 60-second site walkthrough reel per month: filmed by the field team, subtitled by xto100x, posted with a hook-frame text overlay. No production budget required.
